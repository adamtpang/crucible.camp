01What it is

A crucible is where raw material meets enough heat and pressure to become something else.

Same idea, fewer apologies. Crucible is a 36-hour Bitcoin hackathon at Network School — builders, founders, and capital in one room, working on problems that are actually worth solving. Fully documented. Livestreamed start to finish.

02Who it's for

Three kinds of people. One room.

  • BUILDERS

    Ship something real in 36 hours — Bitcoin, Lightning, and the hard problems around them. Bring your hands.

  • FOUNDERS

    Bring the problem worth solving. Leave with a team that solved it, and a thing you can take to market.

  • INVESTORS

    Capital in the room, not on a call. Meet the teams while the code is still warm — before everyone else does.

03What happens

Heat, pressure, and a deadline.

50 teams, selected
36 hours, no reset
1 demo day that decides it

No tracks for the sake of tracks. No filler panels. You build, it gets documented, and on the final day you put it in front of the people who can fund it.

04The backing

Real capital, committed before you arrive.

Backed by a group of Bitcoin-native funds and operators, with capital committed to the teams that ship. Names and numbers announced soon.
